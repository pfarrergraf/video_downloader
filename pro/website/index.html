Do I need Pro to use the app?

No. The app is free and fully usable. Pro removes limits and supports development.

Where can I install DownloadThat?

Google Play is the recommended Android installation. A signed direct APK remains available as a secondary option.

Where do I enter my license key?

After a verified Google Play purchase, the app shows your license key. Enter the same key in the direct APK or Windows app.

Can I get a refund?

Google Play manages payments and eligible refunds. If a purchase is refunded or revoked, the linked Pro license is deactivated automatically.
